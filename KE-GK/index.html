07 — Promotional Materials

Promotional Materials

Print and signage concepts to bring the brands to life at the entrance and throughout the restaurant.

Entrance Roll-Up Banner

Portrait banner for the restaurant entrance. Hotel Indigo branding at the top, "Social Bird — A Curated Kitchen" headline, all five brand names stacked vertically with their brand colours, "Powered by Growth Kitchen" at the footer.

Size: 850mm × 2000mm · Format: Pull-up banner stand

Window Stickers

Circular 20cm diameter stickers for each brand, applied to entrance windows and doors. Each sticker uses the brand's colours and carries the brand name, category, and tagline. An additional "Social Bird" sticker ties the concept together.

Size: 200mm diameter circles · Full-colour vinyl

QR Code Table Cards

Small table-top cards with a QR code linking to the full digital menu. Printed on heavy card stock in Hotel Indigo blue with gold accents. Each table gets one card in an acrylic holder.

Size: A6 (105 × 148mm) · Heavy card, double-sided

Physical Menu

Printed menu booklet with Indigo blue cover and gold foil "Social Bird" title. Inside: double-page spreads per brand using each brand's colours. Brand story on the left, menu items on the right. Back cover has QR code.

Size: A4 landscape, folded · 8 pages · Silk finish

In-Room QR Code (Printed)

Printed card placed in each hotel room with a QR code linking to the room service menu. Branded in Hotel Indigo blue with Social Bird branding. Positioned on the desk or bedside table alongside the hotel compendium.

Size: A6 (105 × 148mm) · Heavy card · One per room

In-Room QR Code (TV Screen)

QR code displayed on the hotel room TV welcome/info screen. Guests can scan directly from bed to browse the room service menu and order. Co-branded with Hotel Indigo and Social Bird.

Format: Digital asset for hotel TV system · 1920×1080px

Elevator Branding

Brand showcase panel inside hotel elevators displaying all five brand logos, a QR code to the menu, and the "Social Bird — A Curated Kitchen" tagline. Captures guests on the way to their room or heading out.

Format: Printed panel or digital screen · Positioned at eye level

Check-In Vouchers

10% discount voucher handed to every guest at check-in. Branded card with a unique code or tear-off strip. Encourages first visit to Social Bird during their stay. Valid for dine-in orders only.

Size: DL (99 × 210mm) · Heavy card · "Welcome to Social Bird — 10% off your first order"
