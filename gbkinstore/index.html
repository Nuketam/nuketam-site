The Story So Far

From delivery success to in-store opportunity

Growth Kitchen has operated GBK across 25+ delivery locations, building the brand's digital presence and reaching new customers every day. In the process, we uncovered something exciting: the demand for GBK extends far beyond delivery — and in-store presence actually fuels more delivery orders.

1 Built the delivery engine

We launched GBK on all major delivery platforms across 25+ locations. Our multi-brand kitchen model means lower overhead, faster rollout, and consistent quality at scale.

2 Discovered in-store demand

Hotel and pub operators we work with consistently ask for dine-in food options. They want recognisable brands that drive footfall and margin — GBK is the perfect fit.

3 Proven the model already works

We've already launched in IHG hotels and have a pipeline of ~20 sites ready to go. Strong operator interest and customer satisfaction from day one.

4 Ready to scale together

We're scaling towards hundreds of locations. Each new site is an incremental revenue stream for GBK — zero capex and zero operational burden on the brand.

The Flywheel Effect

A hotel guest who tries a GBK burger at dinner becomes a repeat delivery customer at home. This is more involved than a typical delivery-only operation, but that's precisely what makes it valuable. Physical presence builds brand awareness that no delivery-app advertising can buy.

The Concept

Market hall meets iconic burgers

Instead of replicating a full GBK restaurant, we're proposing a lighter, modern format designed for shared venues. GBK sits proudly alongside other exceptional brands, giving customers choice while each brand shines.

Traditional Dine-In

  • ● Single-brand, standalone restaurant
  • ● Full kitchen buildout required
  • ● High capex and long lead times
  • ● Complex franchise agreements
  • ● Customer orders from one brand only
  • ● Operator locked into single concept

GBK In-Store by Growth Kitchen

  • ✓ Multi-brand market hall format
  • ✓ Uses existing kitchen infrastructure
  • ✓ Minimal capex, fast deployment
  • ✓ Simple licence extension — no new legals
  • ✓ Customers order across multiple brands seamlessly
  • ✓ Operators get variety and higher margins
The Experience

The GBK standard, maintained

We understand how important brand integrity is. Every in-store location will replicate the GBK dining experience in presentation and quality — not just the food, but how it reaches the customer.

Plating & presentation Key

Food will be plated in a setup comparable to the in-store GBK experience, with branded packaging where appropriate and the same attention to presentation that guests expect from the brand.

Same recipes, same suppliers

Every product uses the same GBK-approved recipes, ingredients, and suppliers. The quality a customer gets at a GBK restaurant is the quality they get at a Growth Kitchen venue. No compromises.

How It Works

Clear roles, full oversight

The model is built on proven partnerships. The hotel or pub provides the space. A trained operator runs the day-to-day. Growth Kitchen provides full oversight. GBK provides the brand.

1

Day-to-day operations

The hotel's own kitchen team or one of our proven delivery-only partners operates the kitchen daily. These are operators we've already trained and vetted across 25+ sites — they know the GBK menu inside out.

2

Growth Kitchen oversight

We provide full operational oversight: supply chain management, staff training, quality audits, and ongoing support. We're the layer that ensures consistency across every location, every day.

3

Venue provides the space

Hotels and pubs provide the kitchen and front-of-house. Minimal fit-out required. We work within their existing infrastructure — no major construction, no long lead times.

4

Customers order & enjoy

Guests order via QR code, tablet, or counter. Food is plated to a dine-in standard — served properly and presented the way GBK customers expect.

Brand Protection

Only in pre-approved, brand-aligned venues

We won't put GBK just anywhere. Every venue goes through an approval process designed to protect the brand and ensure the right fit. We're aiming for full alignment — not volume for volume's sake.

📍

Venue vetting

Every hotel and pub is assessed for brand fit, kitchen capability, footfall, and guest profile before GBK is introduced.

✅

Pre-approval process

New locations are shared with GBK in advance. No site goes live without the Licensor being informed and having the opportunity to flag concerns.

💪

Ongoing standards

GBK retains the right to inspect any venue at any time. We welcome this — our goal is to make the brand proud, not just present.

Commercials

Same terms, new channel

We see in-store as a natural extension of our delivery relationship. The commercial model stays the same — a brand licence fee tied to net revenue, exactly as per our existing agreement.

The Proposal

In-store sales are treated as an extension of the current delivery licence. The same royalty structure applies, keeping things simple and aligned with the existing partnership.

Zero capex from GBK. Zero operational burden. Pure incremental revenue.

No franchise fees. No new legal framework. No complex training programmes. Just a lightweight amendment to the existing agreement that unlocks a whole new revenue stream for the brand.
